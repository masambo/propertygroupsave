[ Important Information ]

Understand the Terms of Land Ownership

We believe in full transparency. Please review these crucial terms before completing your application form.

  • Unserviced Land: All plots are unserviced land.
  • Water Availability: Water is available within the development.
  • Electricity Status: Electricity has not yet been connected and will be provided during future servicing.
  • Title Deeds: Title deeds are not issued immediately upon purchase.
  • Purchase Agreement: Every buyer receives a legally drafted Purchase Agreement prepared by our lawyer, which secures ownership of the purchased plot until title deeds become available.

[ FAQ ]

Common Questions

What is Property Group Save Namibia?

A land development company focused on helping Namibians access affordable unserviced land plots.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes, a deposit is required to secure your plot. Deposits range from N$1,500 to N$15,000 depending on the development you choose.

Is there an application fee?

Yes, a non-refundable application fee of N$250 applies to all new applications.

What interest rate do you charge?

All of our flexible payment installment plans are structured with 0% interest.

Is this a bank loan?

No. This is a direct purchase plan with Property Group Save Namibia and not a traditional bank mortgage.
