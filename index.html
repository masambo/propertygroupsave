[ About Us ]

Property Group Save Namibia was created to address one of Namibia's biggest challenges

Property Group Save Namibia was created to address one of Namibia's biggest challenges: access to affordable housing and land ownership. We believe housing should be approached with humanity, development, and fairness.

Our goal is to make property ownership more accessible through collective thinking, land development, and affordable housing models.

[ How It Works ]

How It Works

We Acquire Land

We identify and secure land suitable for future residential and commercial development.

We Establish Townships

We work toward proper planning and township establishment to support structured development.

We Service Land

We support the process of preparing land with basic infrastructure for future development.

We Develop Houses

We create affordable housing options for different family sizes and income levels.

We Build Communities

We include commercial nodes and development opportunities so that communities can grow economically.
